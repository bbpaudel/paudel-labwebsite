Our collaborative project titled "NPM1 Mutation Specific Therapeutic Vulnerabilities in Acute Myeloid Leukemia" has been selected as an awardee for Hematologic Malignancies Translational Research Team (HM-TRT) Pilot Funding FY2026 ($25,000). This project is a collaboration between Dr. Francine Garrett-Bakelman, Dr. Subhash Prajapati and P3SysBioLab. Our undergraduate, Beckett Lisle contributed to the proposal and will work on this project.
Congratulations Team!
