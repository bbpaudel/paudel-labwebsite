P3SysBioLab undergraduate, Vicente Carvajal Villegas is awarded a prestigious Harrison Undergraduate Research Fellowship for his project, “Relationship between sphingolipid and STAT5 signaling in AML," for 2026 Summer. He will be working on understanding on how two sphingolipid subtypes in AML have distinct signaling through JAK/STAT pathway. Congratulations, Vicente!
