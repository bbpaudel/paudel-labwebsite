Beckett Lisle presented a poster at the Virginia Drug Discovery Consortium (VaDDC) Symposium 2026, *Ten Years of Drug Discovery and Development in Virginia*, held June 8–9, 2026, at The Inn at Virginia Tech and Skelton Conference Center in Blacksburg, Virginia.

The symposium brought together academic researchers, industry scientists, and trainees from across Virginia to discuss recent advances in drug discovery and development. The poster session provided an opportunity for participants to showcase ongoing research and engage with experts from universities and industry throughout the Commonwealth.

Conference details: [Virginia Drug Discovery Consortium (VaDDC) Symposium 2026](https://vaddc.org/symposium-program-2026/)
